About Us

Built on Craft. Driven by Innovation.

We are a full-service design and manufacturing partner specializing in high-end retail environments — helping the world's leading brands bring their vision to life through craftsmanship, precision, and engineering expertise.

Founded in 1999 by brothers Luis and Francis in a 1,500 sq ft Los Angeles workshop, Niconat was built on a simple but powerful idea — that great manufacturing is about more than producing fixtures. It's about understanding a brand's vision and having the skill, technology, and care to execute it flawlessly.
